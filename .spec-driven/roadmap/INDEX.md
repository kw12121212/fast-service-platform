## Notes

- Milestones 1-5 are complete. Milestones 6-8 close the V1 AI-driven assembly path. Milestones 9-15 build the V2 agile process layer.
- Operational and maintenance changes (e.g. vendor syncs, schema migrations) are archived outside milestone scope and do not affect milestone status.
- V1 hard scope boundaries are documented in `specs/product/v1-scope-boundaries.md`. These apply to all V1 roadmap work.
- V2 agile milestones introduce AI employees as first-class team members. The design principle is: "AI is a team member, not a tool." Every downstream module treats AI employees identically to human members.
- Sprint, Board, and Collaboration milestones (11, 12, 13) can be developed in parallel once work items and teams exist.
